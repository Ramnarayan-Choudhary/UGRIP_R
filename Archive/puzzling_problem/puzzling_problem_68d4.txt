Meta: w=w in win. j=y in yum. ʃ and ʒ are consonants. The marks ˊ and ˋ indicate high and low tone, respectively.
Source and Target:
ŋgámónà dìhónʒò mùdìlóŋgà	:I saw the banana on the plate.
àlóʒí ásáŋgá djálà mùdìkúŋgù	:The sorcerers met the man in the cave.
ŋgádjà dìhónʒó djámì	:I ate my banana.
mùdjúlù mwálà ʒìtéténbwà	:There are stars in the sky.
dìkámbá djámí djáʃíkà nì djákínà	:My friend sang and danced.
ŋgámónà dìkúŋgú djámí	:Did I see my cave?
ŋgámóná málà mùkìtándà	:I saw the men in the square.
ŋgásáŋgá múlóʒí mwámì mùlwándà	:I met my sorcerer in Luanda.
mùkìtándà mwálá djálá djámì	:My (man/husband) is in the square.
mùdìkúŋgù ŋgámónà màkòlómbóló	:Did I see the roosters in the cave?
àtú ádjà dìhónʒò mùlwándá	:Did the people eat the banana in Luanda?

English to kimbundu:
Did I sing?
The person met the sorcerer and the friend in the square.
My (man/husband) saw the cave.
There are sorcerers in Luanda.
